Loans 2 Go Careers

Considering a new career? Want a job with prospects? Loans 2 Go is always on the lookout for talented and ambitious people.


As the market leader in the logbook loans sector, we operate the countries’ largest network of vehicle-secured lending outlets. As an online lender we’re able to offer clients extended hours of support; making Loans 2 Go more accessible and able to help more people find the funding they need. In addition to logbook loans we also offer personal loans for those that may not own a vehicle.


We look to help individuals and small businesses solve their cash flow challenges with either personal loan or a loan secured against their vehicle. As a longstanding member of the CCTA and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority, our Company provides a completely transparent service, giving applicants unique control over the size, length and cost of their loan, whilst making sure it is affordable for them.


This is a people business and our growth to this position in the marketplace has been built upon the successful relationships developed with clients by our friendly well-trained customer service teams,. We want to bring in more of the same; people with great communication skills, problem solvers who are able to show empathy and understanding, those who will work hard with customers to ensure good outcomes from their dealings with us.


If you want to be a part of the Loans 2 Go team, e-mail us now at hr@loans2go.co.uk or s.stina@loans2go.co.uk with a covering letter along with your CV. For information on how we use your data when you apply for a job with us on any of the Jobs website, please read the privacy policy here.


Due to the large numbers of responses we receive & despite our best efforts it is not always possible to respond to every application. Therefore, only short-listed candidates will be contacted for particular roles. If you haven’t heard from us within 10 days please assume you have been unsuccessful on this occasion.
