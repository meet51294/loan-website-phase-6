About Us

Welcome to Step Change. Below you will find all the information about our company, our values, benefits and why treating customers fairly is so important to us.

Step Chnage Values

At Step Change our number one priority will always be to treat all our customers fairly and provide them with the information and assistance they need throughout the duration of their loan agreement with us. In order to demonstrate this commitment, we have developed the following promises which form the basis of our corporate ethos:

1. We want to make sure you’re comfortable with the terms of the agreement before asking you to sign anything. The terms of your agreement will be shown in full before you sign your agreement. You can also contact us any time with any questions you may have.

2. We will do everything we can to ensure any agreement you take out with us fits within your budget. Your loan instalments will be clearly marked in the loan agreement you sign.

3. We understand that circumstances can change. If you are having difficulty making payments, we will work with you to find a solution.

4. We will listen carefully to any complaint or feedback you may have about the Step Change product and we will do our best to put things right.

Step Change Benifits

Fast Personal Loans Online
Our primary loan option available online is our Standard Personal Loan, giving you the money you require within 60 minutes once accepted. We let you borrow from £250 – £1,000 over 18 months (subject to eligibility and affordability), giving you the benefit of applying for a loan from the comfort of your own home.

Payment Frequency
We all get paid at different points during the month and our payment frequency options have been designed to reflect this. We are happy for you to pay weekly, fortnightly or monthly.

Loan Duration
Our online Standard Personal Loans are for 18 months and the best bit is that you can settle your loan at any point during your agreement and pay less interest. You only need to pay the interest for the time you keep the loan.

Other Products
We also provide other loan products including logbook loans. You can apply for other products by phone or online.

Treating Customer Fairly

We make sure the terms of your agreement are shown in full when you apply for a loan. We will also ensure that you are able to afford and pay your instalments. You can also contact us with any questions before you sign the loan agreement.
We are fully authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority, and are proud members of the CCTA abiding by their Bills of Sale Code of Practice.
We also have a dedicated Compliance team, who work hard to ensure that the Step Change product is designed to meet the needs of our customer.
If you need help financially, you can be confident that the fair treatment of customers is central to what we’re trying to achieve at Step Change and we will continue to build our corporate culture around this ethos.
